What is Recur?

Recur is a subscription management and money saving app, whose purpose is to boost the financial awareness, freedom, and savings of its users.

By signing up to Recur, you too will be able to save hundreds of pounds a year! Through our intelligent analytics, you'll be able to find and delete unwanted subscriptions, and also find better alternatives to existing ones!
